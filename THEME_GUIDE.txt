Q: Theme doesn't apply on startup?
A: Make sure you saved the file and restarted Neovim.
   Also check for typos in theme name.

Q: Colors look wrong?
A: Your terminal might not support 24-bit colors.
   Check your terminal's color support settings.

Q: Gruvbox error?
A: This is now fixed! Just use the centralized selector
   in lua/core/theme.lua

Q: Want to change theme without restarting?
A: Use :colorscheme themename
   Then edit lua/core/theme.lua to make it permanent.
